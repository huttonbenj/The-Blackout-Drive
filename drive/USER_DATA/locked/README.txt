LOCKED FILES
================================

Files in this folder are AES-256-GCM encrypted .bkv (Blackout Vault) files,
managed by The Blackout Drive's Workspace interface.

To access these files, open The Blackout Drive and use
the "Locked" tab in the Workspace. You will be
prompted for your master password.

These files are protected by a unified V3 Segmented AEAD
streaming architecture and can only be decrypted through
The Blackout Drive interface.
