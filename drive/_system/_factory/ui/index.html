BLACKOUT PROTOCOL

The toggle in the top-right corner of the screen. This is your one-click security switch.

When Blackout Protocol is ON (the default):
• Network Lock is forced ON — the drive cannot access the internet
• Encrypt Chat History is forced ON — your conversations are encrypted with your master password

You can turn it OFF to control these settings individually in the Settings panel. Turning it off does not make you less secure — both settings stay on until you manually change them. You can turn Blackout Protocol back on at any time.

PASSWORD & ENCRYPTION

When you first use the drive, you create a master password. This one password protects everything that is encrypted:

Encrypted chatsYour conversations are scrambled so only you can read them
Locked filesFiles you upload to the "Locked" tab in the Workspace
Exported backupsConversation exports are encrypted too

Changing your password:
Go to Settings → Data → Change Master Password. This re-encrypts all your data with the new password. Nothing is deleted — your data stays intact.

Forgot your password:
Click "Forgot password?" when you're asked to enter it. ⚠ Warning: This permanently deletes ALL encrypted conversations and locked files. BEACON, the Library, Prompts, unlocked files, and your settings are not affected. There is no way to recover a forgotten password — this is by design for your security.
