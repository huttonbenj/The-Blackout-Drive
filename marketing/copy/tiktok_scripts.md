## General Production Notes

- **Shoot in landscape AND portrait** — TikTok/Reels = 9:16, YouTube Shorts = 9:16, but YouTube long-form = 16:9. Get both.
- **Screen recordings** should be tight-cropped on the browser — don't show the full desktop with visible dock/taskbar.
- **The WiFi-off moment** is the money shot in Script 1. Make it visually dramatic — show the WiFi icon disappearing.
- **Product shots** should be dark/moody. Matte black surface, warm side lighting. The drive should look premium and tactical, not like a $10 USB stick from Best Buy.
- **Never say "doomsday" or "apocalypse"** in the video — too polarizing. Use "blackout," "grid failure," "power outage," "offline." This keeps the content from being labeled as fear-mongering by the algorithm.
- **CTA is always "Link in bio"** on TikTok. On YouTube Shorts, use "Link in description."
